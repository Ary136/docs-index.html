About Ba Oud

Ba Oud is a luxury, small-batch perfume house specializing in oud-based fragrances. Rooted in Middle Eastern tradition and Shia mysticism, our scents evoke sacred nights, noble souls, and the soul of the unseen.

Signature Scents
